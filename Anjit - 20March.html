Introducing Simplified —
an Analyst-in-a-Box for the mid-market.

After years in transaction advisory, I kept seeing the same problem: boutique firms and independent advisors spending hours on financial modelling that should take minutes. Simplified is my answer — an enterprise-grade platform that transforms raw financial data into clean, actionable investment analysis, powered by AI.

$10.97B
Investment Modelling Software market by 2035
Growing at 9.6% CAGR, shifting aggressively toward tools offering fast, dynamic scenario modelling over rigid legacy software. Target users: boutique M&A firms, fractional CFOs, corporate development teams.
🎨

Zen-Finance UI

Minimalist block-based design with a Traffic Light system (Green/Red/Neutral) that replaces dense data tables. Dual modes: Analyst (full inputs) and Presenter (clean client view).

Analyst Mode & Presenter Mode Interactive Tutor Layer White-label PDF exports
⚙️

Logic & Math Engine

Auto-ingests historical financials, cross-references macro APIs (industry growth, regional GDP), and instantly projects 5-year baseline forecasts feeding a live DCF — no manual entry.

Real-time DCF calculations Normalised EBITDA & trading comps Automated scenario modelling

AI & Data Layer

Integrated LLM reads math engine outputs and auto-drafts CIM narratives — Executive Summary, Investment Criteria, Industry Analysis, Risk Identification. Proprietary IP stays on-server.
